HydroNet v1.0.0

Network-Based Hydrological Early Warning System

12-Parameter Monitoring

  • Atmospheric: Precipitation, Evapotranspiration, Pressure
  • Surface Water: Streamflow, Lake Levels, Reservoir Storage
  • Groundwater: Levels, Quality
  • Soil & Land: Moisture, Subsidence
  • Human Impact: Water Extraction, Land Use Change
